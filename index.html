Departments.

There are a lot of things that goes into making a plane, let’s dive into it.

Design


The design phase includes several important tasks such as the design requirement analysis , constraint analysis, aircraft configuration, and the establishment of initial planning for UAV development. The design team decides the airfoil, fuselage, wing, tail, control surfaces and does the stability analysis using various software. Extensive analysis is done to optimise the aircraft aerodynamics

Structures


Structure department consists of 3D CAD modelling, structural integrity and weight reduction. The CAD modelling is done using softwares like Solidworks, Fusion 360 etc. The department uses ANSYS static structural to validate the structural stability of each and every part by varying the size and material. Further, Topology Optimization is used to find out zero stress areas in order to reduce empty weight of the plane.

CFD


Computational Fluid Dynamics is a branch of fluid mechanics that uses numerical analysis and data structures to solve problems related to fluid flows. For CFD analysis the department uses ANSYS Fluent to compute the Lift, Drag and Pressure related parameters of the airfoil as well as the plane and to optimise the design of the fuselage to reduce Drag.

Avioinics


Avionics are the electronic systems used on aircraft. Avionics team selects the motor, propeller, battery ESC and receiver based on the requirements . The team does software and experimental analysis to obtain the best-suited drive train for the aircraft

Management

The management team get sponsors for the team and promote the team as much as possible. The management takes care of all the documents and permissions needed for an event or even participation in a competition.
